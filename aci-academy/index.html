ACI Academy

Académie ACI

ACI 学院

Develop the five creative capacities of Agentic Creative Intelligence to ensure your engagement with AI is meaningful. Assess where you stand, be inspired by a role model, and generate a personalised Creative Council to guide your creative collaboration with any AI.

Développez les cinq capacités créatives de l'Intelligence Créative Agentique pour garantir que votre engagement avec l'IA soit significatif. Évaluez votre niveau, inspirez-vous d'un modèle, et générez un Conseil Créatif personnalisé pour guider votre collaboration créative avec toute IA.

培养能动创造智能的五种创造力，确保您与AI的互动富有意义。评估现状，从榜样中获得启发，生成个人创意理事会来指导您与任何AI的创造性协作。

AI makes prompting frictionless, and cognitive retreat effortless. The ACI Academy aims to reclaim human agency with practices for the agentic era.

L'IA rend le prompting sans friction et le retrait cognitif sans effort. L'Académie ACI vise à reconquérir l'agentivité humaine avec des pratiques pour l'ère agentique.

AI 让提示变得无摩擦，让认知退缩变得毫不费力。ACI 学院旨在通过智能体时代的实践重塑人类能动性。

The Five Capacities Les cinq capacités 五种能力
🔍
Extended Engagement
Depth & Reach
The capacity to stay immersed in a problem long enough to understand it deeply. AI delivers answers before you have struggled enough to learn.
👁️
Aesthetic Awareness
Perception & Form
The capacity to perceive what matters through the body before the mind categorises it. AI rates and selects before you have registered what you feel.
🧭
Adaptive Intention
Understanding & Change
The capacity to hold purpose steady while adjusting to new information. AI optimises before you have understood the problem deeply enough.
📣
Expressive Influence
Agency & Creation
The capacity to act with conviction and shape outcomes through creative expression. AI generates before you have found your own voice.
◆
ACI Convergence
Integration & Ecology
The integration of all four capacities into an ecology of creative practice. AI fragments what should be held together.
🔍
Engagement Étendu
Profondeur & Portée
La capacité de rester immergé dans un problème assez longtemps pour le comprendre en profondeur. L'IA fournit des réponses avant que vous n'ayez suffisamment lutté pour apprendre.
👁️
Conscience Esthétique
Perception & Forme
La capacité de percevoir ce qui compte à travers le corps avant que l'esprit ne catégorise. L'IA sélectionne avant que vous n'ayez ressenti.
🧭
Intention Adaptative
Compréhension & Changement
La capacité de maintenir un objectif stable tout en s'adaptant aux nouvelles informations. L'IA optimise avant que vous n'ayez compris le problème.
📣
Influence Expressive
Action & Création
La capacité d'agir avec conviction et de façonner les résultats par l'expression créative. L'IA génère avant que vous n'ayez trouvé votre voix.
◆
Convergence ACI
Intégration & Écologie
L'intégration des quatre capacités en une écologie de pratique créative. L'IA fragmente ce qui devrait rester uni.
🔍
延展参与
深度与广度
在问题中保持足够深入沉浸的能力。AI在你充分探索之前就给出了答案。
👁️
审美意识
感知与形式
在思维分类之前通过身体感知重要事物的能力。AI在你感受之前就已经筛选和评判。
🧭
适应性意图
理解与变化
在适应新信息的同时保持目标稳定的能力。AI在你深入理解问题之前就已优化。
📣
表达影响力
能动性与创造
以信念行动并通过创造性表达塑造结果的能力。AI在你找到自己声音之前就已生成。
◆
ACI 融合
整合与生态
将四种能力整合为创造性实践生态的能力。AI将本应统一的东西碎片化。

Refik Anadol
Digital Art · Data Visualisation
Represent Intentions Tinker with Tech
Anadol curates AI outputs into immersive spatial experiences — feeding 200 years of art history into fluid visualisations at MoMA. Studying his practice develops your capacity to represent creative intentions through technology while keeping human aesthetic judgment in command.
Holly Herndon
Music · AI Collaboration
Represent Intentions Cultivate Opportunities
Herndon trained Holly+ on her own vocal tradition — liturgical singing, choral practice — creating a decentralised voice instrument governed by a community of stewards. Her work models how to cultivate new creative opportunities while maintaining vocal sovereignty.
Sougwen Chung
Performance Art · Robotics
Represent Intentions Tinker with Tech
Chung has performed with D.O.U.G. — a robotic arm that mirrors and counters their drawing movements — since 2015, treating machine departures as native vocabulary of a different sensory architecture. Their practice strengthens your ability to represent intentions through embodied human-machine dialogue.
Wayne McGregor
Choreography · AI
Represent Intentions Cognitive Awareness
McGregor uses AI to generate movement possibilities from video archives of his company's repertoire — then selects, rejects, and recombines through embodied judgment. His practice develops cognitive awareness of how decisions shape choreographic meaning.
Ganit Goldstein
Textile · Wearable Technology
Tinker with Tech Represent Intentions
Goldstein's collaboration with MIT on spacesuit textiles produces a single continuous weave with millimetre-by-millimetre variable properties — holding computational precision in tension with awareness of bodies that sweat and flex. Her work teaches how technology extends rather than replaces embodied design.
Matias del Campo
Architecture · Neural Aesthetics
Future Focus Represent Intentions
Del Campo uses neural networks to remix architectural ornament into post-human forms that estrange the familiar. His practice at SPAN Studio develops your future focus — the ability to envision and represent directions that spark entirely new innovation trajectories.
Sasha Stiles
Poetry · AI
Represent Intentions Cognitive Awareness
Stiles trained AI on her own poetry corpus and discovered the collaboration produces a "third voice" — one requiring the poet's body to carry meaning. Her practice develops cognitive awareness of how human and machine language interact, and the ability to represent poetic intention through hybrid authorship.
Jiajian Min
Performance · Multimedia · AI/XR
Represent Intentions Tinker with Tech
Min integrates dance, calligraphy, projection mapping, and generative systems — translating Chinese calligraphy stroke-body motion into generative visuals through motion capture and biometric feedback. His practice deepens embodied creative reach through computational tools without severing the body's authority.
Jacob Collier
Music · Production · Community
Represent Intentions Social Orchestration
Collier builds layered harmonic worlds from his bedroom, orchestrating global collaborations and audience participation into complex musical architectures. His practice develops your ability to represent sophisticated creative intentions through technology while orchestrating social situations that build shared meaning from the ground up.
David Jhave Johnston
Digital Poetry · AI
Represent Intentions Mindful Encouragement
Johnston generated 4,500 poems with neural networks, then carved them by hand into twelve volumes over twelve months — proving that curation is authorship. His practice develops your ability to represent creative intentions through AI while maintaining the mindful resilience required to sustain a long creative arc.
Antoni Gaudí
Architecture · Organic Structure
Represent Intentions Future Focus
Gaudí derived structural logic from natural forms — catenary arches, hyperboloid geometries, bone-like branching — decades before computation could model them. His practice develops your ability to represent creative intentions through material intelligence and to envision futures that reconcile engineering with organic life.
Zaha Hadid
Architecture · Parametric Design
Represent Intentions Cultivate Opportunities
Hadid's paintings preceded her buildings — radical spatial visions that no existing technology could realise until computational tools caught up. Her practice develops your ability to represent intentions that exceed current possibility and to cultivate opportunities through sheer force of creative conviction.
Neri Oxman
Material Ecology · Design Science
Cultivate Opportunities Create the Environment
Oxman pioneered Material Ecology — design operating simultaneously at cellular and macroscopic scales, treating materials as programmable substrates. Studying her work develops your capacity to cultivate opportunities by thinking holistically and to create environments that encourage innovative action.
John Maeda
Design · AI · Technology
Future Focus Cultivate Opportunities
Maeda advocates the shift from User Experience to Agent Experience, positioning designers as orchestrators of agent goals and guardrails. His practice develops your future focus and your capacity to cultivate new innovation opportunities at the convergence of design and technology.
Simone Rebaudengo
Product Design · Artisanal Intelligence
Innovator's Identity Tinker with Tech
Rebaudengo's Oio Studio brings AI-generated hallucinations to Pampaloni — a silversmithing atelier founded in 1702 — where master craftspeople determine which curves can actually exist in metal. His approach develops your innovator's identity: fusing craft tradition with computational tools.
François Chartier
Molecular Gastronomy · Computational Cuisine
Cognitive Awareness Cultivate Opportunities
Chartier develops computational gastronomy principles — using molecular analysis to discover flavour harmonies invisible to intuition alone. His practice develops cognitive awareness of how sensory data shapes creative decisions and the ability to cultivate opportunities through scientific rigour.
Fumito Ueda
Game Design · Interactive Narrative
Represent Intentions Mindful Encouragement
Ueda's philosophy — expressed through ICO, Shadow of the Colossus, and The Last Guardian — centres on constraint as emotional investment. Moments given once and never repeated create weight. Rie Kudan

Sean Michaels
Fiction · Hybrid Authorship

Paola Antonelli
Museum Curation · Design

Annie Dorsen
