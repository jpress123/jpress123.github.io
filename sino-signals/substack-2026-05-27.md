## China's AI Four Little Dragons Cross a Combined One Trillion Yuan Valuation

MiniMax sits at HK$257.3 billion (about 223.5 billion yuan), nearly fourfold its IPO price. Moonshot is past 20 billion US dollars (about 136.2 billion yuan) on a 2 billion US dollar round led by Meituan Longzhu.
