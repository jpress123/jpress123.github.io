What does it mean when a Chinese OEM that sold three million cars last year shows up at the Beijing Auto Show with a purpose-built robotaxi and a vehicle-level AI super-agent on a 2027 commercial calendar?

The CaoCao Mobility customised edition launches in 2027, building on pilot operations already running in Hangzhou and Suzhou.
