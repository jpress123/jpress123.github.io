## Cyberspace Administration of China Launches Four-Month Qinglang Campaign Against AI Application Chaos

Stage 1 targets seven source-side problems: failure to file large models under the Generative AI Services Interim Measures, weak safety review and content filtering, training-corpus security, AI data poisoning via generative search engine optimization manipulation, incomplete AIGC labeling under the March 2025 Labeling Measures, misuse of agent and digital-human technology including unauthorized "face-swap and voice-swap" services, and lax open-source community governance. Penalties extend to accounts, MCN agencies, and host platforms.
