Octo plugs directly into Seedance 2.0, the model that topped the Artificial Analysis Video Arena at Elo 1,269, and into Seedream 5.0 Lite. The full pipeline from story outline to short film output runs in one workspace, with the agent perceiving screen state and acting in parallel while the creator types.
