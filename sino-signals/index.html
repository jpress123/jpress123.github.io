About This Project

My Take

China is the world's largest signal amplifier for AI deployment. While frontier research grabs headlines in the West, the signals that will reshape your industry over the next 3–5 years are appearing in China first—not because China is ahead in foundational research, but because it operates at a scale and speed of deployment that has no Western equivalent.

Sino-Signals applies the signal scanning methodology developed at the Institute for the Future (IFTF) to Chinese-language sources. Each signal is evaluated through four questions:

What is the signal? — What happened, with verified sourcing.
Why can it scale in China? — Structural factors enabling rapid deployment.
How might it impact the world? — Second- and third-order effects.
So what would you do Monday morning? — Actionable response for leaders.

Methodology: IFTF Foresight Essentials signal scanning, applied to Chinese-language sources including government portals, industry media (36Kr, CLS, Caixin), WeChat official accounts, and research databases.
