Expertise

Three domains for the AI era

Domain One

Strategic Foresight for Impact

Imagine — Design — Lead

Be future-ready for agents everywhere — across industries, across sectors, across functions. Applied futures thinking for strategic decision-making: using generative AI for scenario simulation, stakeholder engagement, and policy forecasting so leaders can anticipate change and act on it. The Futureback leadership framework guides organizations from a desired future backward to present-day action, turning foresight into strategy with positive impact.

Research Advisor — Institute for the Future (IFTF)

Full Professor — Shanghai University of Engineering Sciences
Frameworks: Imagine-Design-Lead Futureback leadership, Augmented Design (Generate Insights, Extend Reality, Connect Experiences, Distribute Impact), six AI opportunity spaces for organizational strategy, Spectrum of Agility and flexive intent

AI–Human Collaboration for Innovation

Spark and augment innovation capacity

AI-human collaboration that goes beyond new products and services to include education, applied research, and market creation. The Agentic Creative Intelligence framework provides governance structures and diagnostic tools for how teams adopt AI while preserving judgment, accountability, and the capacity to innovate. Applicable to executive education, R&D, faculty development, and cross-functional innovation programs.

Domain Three

AI Transformation for Integration

AI at the core of every offering, process, and function

AI needs to be integrated into every offering, every process, every functional unit. That requires use-case identification, process mapping, change management, and deep understanding of existing organizational structure and IT infrastructure — from ERP to AI. 15 years founding and directing a digital agency and transformation consulting practice, delivering enterprise-scale change across HR, operations, administration, and strategy. Research on why 50%+ of digital transformations stall and engagement-centered models to prevent it.

Founder & Director — Deloitte Digital Switzerland (EMEA)
Executive Education Advisor — Center for Creative Leadership
Co-Founder — Make Our Future (global consulting cooperative)
Innovation center advisory — GovLab, Center for the Edge, Federal Innovation
Capabilities: Use-case identification, process mapping and redesign, change management (visioning, leadership alignment, communications, training), HR transformation, service design, technology adoption strategy, HRIS/BPO, innovation labs
