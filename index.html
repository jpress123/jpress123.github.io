Capability One能力一

AI-Human Collaboration for Accelerated Innovation

人机协作加速创新

Work with agents for greater creativity

与智能体合作实现更大创意

Agentic Creative Intelligence argues that authentic creativity is even more possible and potent with AI. Most organizations adopt AI faster than they build the capacity to use it well. ACI grounds our intelligence in real practices and platforms to creatively intervene in complex situations — with clarity of expression, embodied judgment, and more meaningful experiences. The book introduces the Creative Council: a model where the human practitioner leads and AI collaborates, so that computational reach never replaces the judgment it was designed to extend.

Agentic Creative Intelligence认为真实的创意与AI合作会更加可能和强大。大多数组织采用AI的速度快于建立良好使用AI的能力。ACI将我们的智能扎根于真实的实践和平台中，以创意的方式干预复杂情况——具有表达的清晰性、体现的判断力和更有意义的体验。该书介绍了创意委员会模式：人类从业者领导，AI协作，这样计算能力永远不会替代它被设计来扩展的判断。

Capability Three能力三

Design-Driven Entrepreneurship to Start & Scale OPCs

设计驱动创业初创与扩展

Build, operate, and scale with AI as co-founder

与AI合伙人一起建立、运营和扩展

The One Person Company (OPC) is the AI-native venture model — a single founder with the reach of an entire team. A Design Driven Guide for Entrepreneurs grounds this in strategic design as a mindset for framing problems, identifying opportunities, and creating pathways forward at every stage of growth.

一人公司(OPC)是AI原生创业模式——一个创始人拥有整个团队的触及范围。A Design Driven Guide for Entrepreneurs以战略设计为基础，作为框架问题、识别机会和在每个增长阶段创建前进路径的思维方式。

AI Integration for Systemic Transformation

AI整合系统性转型

AI at the core of every offering, process, and function

AI融入每项业务、流程和职能的核心

Over half of digital transformations underperform because technology outpaces organizational readiness. Office Shock provides frameworks with diagnostic tools that help teams achieve purpose with greater judgment, accountability, and human agency while accelerating transformation.

超过一半的数字化转型表现不佳，因为技术超过了组织的就绪程度。Office Shock提供了诊断工具框架，帮助团队在加速转型的同时以更大的判断力、责任感和人类代理来实现目标。

Capability Five能力五

Collective Intelligence for Future-Ready Leadership

集体智能未来就绪领导力

Where people and AI learn to think together

人与AI学会一起思考的地方

Innovation leadership fails when engagement is missing. Drawing on research with seven global companies, IDeaLs shows how corporate university programs and executive education become the proving ground for leaders who harness collective intelligence — the combined capacity of people and AI to think, decide, and act together.

当缺乏参与时，创新领导力失败。基于与七家全球公司的研究，IDeaLs展示了公司大学项目和高管教育如何成为利用集体智能的领导者的试验场——人与AI思考、决策和一起行动的综合能力。
