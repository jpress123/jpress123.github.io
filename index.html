About

Two decades building the bridge
from digital transformation to AI

My career has followed a clear trajectory: from leading digital transformation at scale to directing research and practice on how organizations adopt AI without eroding judgment, accountability, or institutional knowledge.

2001 – 2017

Digital Transformation at Scale

Founded Deloitte Digital Switzerland and built a 70+ person EMEA transformation consulting practice. Redesigned executive education at the Center for Creative Leadership. Co-designed graduate programs at Parsons. Delivered innovation labs, organizational change management, process redesign, and talent strategy for global enterprises across HR, operations, and business strategy.

2018 – Present

AI-Driven Transformation

Directing research on AI–human collaboration at the Institute for the Future and Copenhagen Institute for Futures Studies. Developing frameworks for how generative and agentic AI can augment the capabilities of leaders, faculty, and administrative teams. Teaching organizational strategy and AI-augmented pedagogy at Shanghai University of Engineering Sciences. Author of eight books.
